Why mobile?

When you put your brand on a billboard, it stays in one place. Billboards are only allowed in specific places, governed by LUB(Land use bylaw). When you advertise on our trailers, your brand reaches places where billboards are not allowed, this is huge when trying to reach commuters on the busiest highways.

Between Calgary and Edmonton (QEII / Highway 2 corridor), you get:

  • Daily commuter traffic
  • Commercial fleet traffic
  • Intercity travelers
  • Weekend traffic

Instead of buying:

  • A Calgary billboard
  • An Edmonton billboard

One campaign can cover both cities + everything in between!
